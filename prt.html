PERSONALISED RAPID TRANSIT

Good things come in small packages. Our PRTs are designed to have a high frequency of operation and will consume only a third of the energy per passenger as compared to other transportation systems.

PRT PLANNING & ENGINEERING
PRT operates very differently from other transit systems and transit planners and engineers, should elaborate knowledge necessary to unlock its full benefits. Correctly implementing a PRT positioning requires in-depth understanding of:

  • - Short headway possibilities and limitations
  • - Speed possibilities and limitations
  • - Off-board Fare Collection
  • - Ride-sharing methodologies
  • - Corridor vs. networked alignments
  • - Station spacing pros and cons
  • - Empty vehicle management
  • - Maintenance/storage and control room requirements
  • - Safety and security requirements, etc.
